Urban Gardening

Create your own tiny garden at home

Fantastic Features

& growing made easy.

The Usprout tiny garden is a modern hydroponic garden system that allows you to grow large indoor plants. Canadian made, this indoor garden makes vertical farming at home possible and beautifully complements your home. The Usprout tiny garden can grow over 200 different plants, and up to 10 lbs of fresh, sustainable and pesticide-free produce on a monthly basis.
